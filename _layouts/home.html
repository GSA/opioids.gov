Confronting the opioid crisis in the United States

Last year, more Americans died due to opioid overdoses than car crashes. From cities and suburbs to rural America, opioid addiction is the epidemic next door. This website illustrates both the magnitude of the opioid crisis and the actions the Trump Administration is taking to address it.

Preventing Addiction Through Safer Prescribing

Over-prescribing of opioids has contributed to rising rates of opioid addiction and overdoses. Working with health care providers and patients, the Trump Administration is working to drive down both the number and strength of opioid prescriptions and advance evidence-based pain care.
